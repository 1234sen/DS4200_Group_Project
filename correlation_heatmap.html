Correlation Heatmap of Numerical Variables Including BMI

This interactive heatmap visualizes the pairwise correlations among numerical variables in the obesity dataset, including a dynamically calculated Body Mass Index (BMI). The chart uses red tones to indicate strong positive correlations, blue tones for strong negative correlations, and neutral colors for weak or no relationships, with annotations showing exact correlation coefficients. Findings - Correlation Heatmap
