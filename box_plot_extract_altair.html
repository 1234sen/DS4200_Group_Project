Box Plot of Age Distribution by Obesity Level (Altair)

Findings - Box Plot

  • Individuals with insufficient weight and normal weight tend to be younger, as seen by lower median ages.
  • Obesity Type I and Type II categories have higher median ages, suggesting obesity may be more prevalent in older individuals.
  • Overweight Level II shows a wide age spread and the highest observed ages among the groups, indicating a broader impact.
  • Obesity Type III exhibits a smaller interquartile range with a cluster toward younger ages when compared to Types I and II.
  • There are visible outliers in almost all categories, indicating significant age variability even within the same obesity level.
